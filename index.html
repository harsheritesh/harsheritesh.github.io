Hi! My name is Ritesh Harshe. I am a PhD scholar at the International Centre for Theoretical Sciences, Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (ICTS-TIFR), Bangalore. I am working in the gravitational-wave (GW) data analysis. I am involved in the search of lensing in the GW observations, and the development of deep learning methods for early warning of GW signals for follow-up of electromagnetic transients. I am happy to also work in other areas of GW data analysis.
